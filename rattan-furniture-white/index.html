Easy to Maintain

White rattan furniture is not only stylish and durable but also remarkably easy to maintain. Its smooth surface and natural resistance to dirt and moisture make cleaning a breeze.

For regular cleaning, simply wipe down your white rattan furniture with a damp cloth. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners, as these can damage the finish of the rattan.

If your white rattan furniture does get dirty or stained, you can use a mild soap solution to gently clean it. Simply mix a few drops of mild dish soap in a bowl of warm water and use a soft cloth to wipe down the furniture. Rinse thoroughly with a clean damp cloth to remove any soap residue.

For tougher stains, you can try using a mixture of lemon juice and salt. Apply the mixture to the stain and let it sit for a few minutes before wiping it away with a clean damp cloth. Rinse thoroughly to remove any residue.
