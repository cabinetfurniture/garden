Ercol Dressers UK The History and Legacy of Classic British Furniture

Ercol is a British furniture brand known for its timeless and functional pieces. The company was founded in 1920 by Lucian Ercolani, an Italian immigrant who settled in High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire. Ercol's early designs were inspired by the modernist movement, and the company quickly gained a reputation for producing high-quality, well-made furniture that was both stylish and affordable.

Today, Ercol is still producing furniture that is both stylish and functional. The company's dressers are a perfect example of this, combining classic design with modern functionality. Ercol dressers are made from solid wood and feature dovetail joints, which ensure durability and longevity.

Ercol dressers are available in a variety of styles, from traditional to contemporary. The company's most popular dresser is the Windsor, which was first introduced in 1950. The Windsor is a simple yet elegant dresser that features a combination of solid wood and veneered panels. The dresser is available in a variety of finishes, including oak, walnut, and cherry.
