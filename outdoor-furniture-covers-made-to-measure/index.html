Outdoor Furniture Covers Made To Measure

Written by Daniel Aug 01, 2024 · 5 min read

When it comes to protecting your outdoor furniture from the elements, a well-fitting cover is essential. But finding a cover that fits perfectly can be a challenge, especially if your furniture is an unusual size or shape.

That's where made-to-measure furniture covers come in. These covers are designed to fit your furniture exactly, providing the best possible protection from rain, sun, and wind. They're also custom-made, so you can choose the perfect color and fabric to match your décor.

In this article, we'll take a closer look at made-to-measure furniture covers and discuss the benefits of choosing this type of cover for your outdoor furniture.

Covers made to measure

Covers made to measure offer a number of advantages over standard covers.

  • Perfect fit
  • Customizable
  • Durable
  • Stylish
  • Convenient
  • Affordable

With so many benefits, it's easy to see why covers made to measure are becoming increasingly popular.

Perfect fit

One of the biggest benefits of made-to-measure covers is their perfect fit. Standard covers are often too large or too small, leaving your furniture exposed to the elements. But a made-to-measure cover will fit your furniture like a glove, providing the best possible protection.

A perfect fit is important for several reasons. First, it helps to prevent the cover from flapping in the wind, which can damage the cover and your furniture. Second, a snug fit helps to keep out rain, snow, and dust. And third, a well-fitting cover will look better on your furniture than a loose or baggy cover.

If you're not sure how to measure your furniture for a made-to-measure cover, don't worry. Most companies that sell made-to-measure covers will provide you with instructions on how to do so. You can also ask a friend or family member to help you.

Once you have your measurements, you can order your made-to-measure cover online or from a local retailer. Made-to-measure covers are typically more expensive than standard covers, but they're worth the investment if you want the best possible protection for your furniture.

Customizable

Another big advantage of made-to-measure covers is that they are customizable. You can choose the perfect color, fabric, and style to match your décor. You can also add features such as vents, zippers, and pockets.

  • Color: Made-to-measure covers are available in a wide range of colors, so you can find the perfect match for your furniture and décor.
  • Fabric: Made-to-measure covers are made from a variety of fabrics, including canvas, polyester, and vinyl. Each fabric has its own advantages and disadvantages, so you can choose the one that best suits your needs.
  • Style: Made-to-measure covers can be made in a variety of styles, from traditional to contemporary. You can also choose to add features such as piping, trim, and embroidery.
  • Vents: Vents help to prevent condensation from building up inside the cover. This is important, especially in humid climates.
  • Zippers: Zippers make it easy to remove and replace the cover. This is important for cleaning and maintenance.
  • Pockets: Pockets can be added to the cover for storing small items such as cushions and pillows.

With so many customization options available, you can create a made-to-measure cover that is perfect for your needs.

Durable

Made-to-measure covers are also very durable. They are made from high-quality materials that are designed to withstand the elements. This means that your cover will last for many years to come, even if it is exposed to harsh weather conditions.

Here are some of the factors that contribute to the durability of made-to-measure covers:

  • High-quality materials: Made-to-measure covers are made from high-quality materials such as canvas, polyester, and vinyl. These materials are resistant to fading, tearing, and mildew.
  • Reinforced seams: The seams on made-to-measure covers are reinforced with double stitching. This helps to prevent the seams from tearing, even under stress.
  • UV protection: Made-to-measure covers are treated with UV protectants. This helps to prevent the cover from fading in the sun.
  • Waterproof: Made-to-measure covers are waterproof. This means that they will keep your furniture dry, even in the heaviest rain.

With proper care and maintenance, your made-to-measure cover will last for many years to come.

Stylish

Made-to-measure covers are also very stylish. They are available in a wide range of colors and fabrics, so you can find the perfect match for your décor. You can also add features such as piping, trim, and embroidery to create a truly unique look.

  • Color: Made-to-measure covers are available in a wide range of colors, so you can find the perfect match for your furniture and décor.
  • Fabric: Made-to-measure covers are made from a variety of fabrics, including canvas, polyester, and vinyl. Each fabric has its own unique look and feel, so you can choose the one that best suits your style.
  • Style: Made-to-measure covers can be made in a variety of styles, from traditional to contemporary. You can also choose to add features such as piping, trim, and embroidery to create a truly unique look.
  • Accessories: You can also add accessories to your made-to-measure cover, such as cushions and pillows. This is a great way to add a personal touch to your outdoor space.

With so many customization options available, you can create a made-to-measure cover that is both stylish and functional.

Convenient

Made-to-measure covers are also very convenient. They are easy to install and remove, and they can be folded up and stored away when not in use. This makes them a great option for people who don't have a lot of storage space.

Here are some of the factors that contribute to the convenience of made-to-measure covers:

  • Easy to install: Made-to-measure covers are easy to install. Simply place the cover over your furniture and secure it with the straps or ties.
  • Easy to remove: Made-to-measure covers are also easy to remove. Simply untie the straps or ties and lift the cover off of your furniture.
  • Easy to store: Made-to-measure covers can be folded up and stored away when not in use. This makes them a great option for people who don't have a lot of storage space.
  • Easy to clean: Made-to-measure covers are easy to clean. Simply wipe them down with a damp cloth or hose them off with water.

With so many convenient features, made-to-measure covers are a great option for people who want to protect their outdoor furniture without a lot of hassle.
