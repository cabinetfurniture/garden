Question 1: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer: The best material for garden furniture depends on your needs and preferences. Teak is a popular choice because it is durable and weather-resistant. Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer: When choosing the right size garden furniture, you need to consider the size of your garden and the number of people you will be using it with.
