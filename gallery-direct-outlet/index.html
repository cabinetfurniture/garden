### Tips

Here are a few tips to help you choose and care for your garden furniture:

**_Tip 1: Choose the right material._**
The material of your garden furniture will have a big impact on its durability and appearance. For example, resin furniture is weather-resistant and easy to clean, while wood furniture is more traditional and requires more maintenance. **_Tip 2: Consider your needs._**
Think about how you'll be using your garden furniture before you buy it. If you have a large family, you'll need furniture that is durable and can accommodate a lot of people. If you have a small yard, you'll need furniture that is compact and easy to move around. **_Tip 3: Measure your space._**
Before you buy garden furniture, measure the space where you plan to put it. This will help you choose furniture that is the right size and scale for your yard. **_Tip 4: Protect your furniture._**
To extend the lifespan of your garden furniture, protect it from the elements. Cover it with a tarp during the winter months, and store it indoors if possible.
