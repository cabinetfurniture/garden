Safety features

Garden furniture fire pit tables are designed with a number of safety features to help prevent accidents. These features include:

  • Spark screens: Spark screens help to prevent embers from escaping from the fire pit and causing a fire. They are typically made from metal or mesh, and they fit over the top of the fire bowl.
  • Safety rings: Safety rings are metal rings that surround the fire bowl. They help to prevent people from accidentally touching the hot fire bowl.
  • Auto-ignition systems: Auto-ignition systems allow you to start the fire pit with the push of a button. This eliminates the need for matches or lighters, which can be dangerous.
  • Flame adjustment controls: Flame adjustment controls allow you to control the height and intensity of the flame. This is important for safety, as it allows you to keep the fire under control.

In addition to these built-in safety features, there are a few things you can do to help prevent accidents when using a garden furniture fire pit table:

  • Never leave a fire pit unattended.
  • Keep children and pets away from the fire pit.
  • Do not use the fire pit in windy conditions.
  • Do not overload the fire pit with fuel.
  • Extinguish the fire completely before leaving it unattended.
