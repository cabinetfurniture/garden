Exceptional strength

Furniture grade plywood is renowned for its exceptional strength, making it an ideal choice for a wide range of structural and load-bearing applications:

  • Multi-ply construction: Furniture grade plywood is made up of multiple layers of wood veneers glued together with alternating grain directions. This cross-ply construction distributes stress evenly, resulting in superior strength and resistance to warping and bending.
  • High-quality veneers: The veneers used in furniture grade plywood are carefully selected for their strength and durability. This ensures that the finished product can withstand heavy loads and demanding use.
  • Advanced adhesives: The adhesives used to bond the veneers together are specially formulated to provide maximum strength and longevity. This ensures that your plywood will maintain its structural integrity over time.
  • Wide range of thicknesses: Furniture grade plywood is available in a variety of thicknesses, allowing you to choose the perfect option for your specific project requirements. Whether you need a thin plywood for paneling or a thicker plywood for furniture frames, you'll find what you need.

The combination of these factors makes furniture grade plywood an excellent choice for projects where strength and durability are paramount, such as cabinetry, furniture, and even structural applications.
