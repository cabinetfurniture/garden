Unlike traditional wood furniture, which can rot, warp, or crack when exposed to moisture, composite furniture is made from materials that are resistant to water, UV rays, and extreme temperatures.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture:

Question 1: What is the best type of garden furniture?
Answer 1: The best type of garden furniture depends on your individual needs and preferences. However, some of the most popular types of garden furniture include teak, wicker, metal, and plastic. Teak is a durable and long-lasting wood that is resistant to rot and decay. Wicker is a natural material that is lightweight and easy to move around. Metal is a strong and sturdy material that is available in a variety of styles. Plastic is a low-maintenance material that is weather-resistant and easy to clean.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer 2: When choosing the right size garden furniture, you need to consider the size of your garden and the number of people you typically entertain. You also need to think about how you plan to use the furniture. If you plan to use it for dining, you will need a table and chairs that are the right size for your guests. If you plan to use it for relaxing, you may want to choose a comfortable sofa or lounge chairs.

Question 3: What is the best way to care for garden furniture?
Answer 3: The best way to care for garden furniture depends on the material it is made from. Teak furniture should be cleaned with a mild soap and water solution and then oiled regularly. Wicker furniture should be brushed regularly to remove dirt and debris. Metal furniture should be wiped down with a damp cloth and then dried thoroughly. Plastic furniture can be cleaned with a mild soap and water solution.

Question 4: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer 4: There are a few things you can do to protect your garden furniture from the elements. You can use a furniture cover, store the furniture in a shed or garage during the off-season, or apply a protective sealant to the furniture.

Question 5: Where can I find garden furniture?
Answer 5: You can find garden furniture at a variety of places, including home improvement stores, department stores, and online retailers. You can also find used garden furniture at garage sales and thrift stores.

Question 6: How much does garden furniture cost?
Answer 6: The cost of garden furniture varies depending on the material, size, and style of the furniture. You can expect to pay anywhere from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars for a set of garden furniture.
