Ducal Furniture was founded in the early 20th century by a group of Italian immigrants who brought their Old World craftsmanship to the United States. The company quickly gained a reputation for producing high-quality furniture that was both stylish and durable. By the mid-20th century, Ducal Furniture had become one of the largest furniture manufacturers in the country, with factories in several states and a workforce of thousands.

However, the furniture industry underwent a major transformation in the late 20th century. The rise of mass-produced, low-cost furniture from overseas led to a decline in demand for high-quality, American-made furniture. FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture:

Question 1: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer: The best material for garden furniture depends on your needs and preferences. Wood is a classic choice that is both durable and stylish. Metal is another durable option that is also easy to clean. Plastic is a more affordable option that is also lightweight and weather-resistant.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer: When choosing the right size garden furniture, it is important to consider the size of your garden and the number of people you will be entertaining. You should also think about how you will be using the furniture. If you will be using it for dining, you will need to make sure that it is large enough to accommodate your guests. If you will be using it for relaxing, you may want to choose something that is more comfortable.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer: The best way to care for your garden furniture is to clean it regularly. You should also protect it from the elements by covering it when it is not in use. If your furniture is made of wood, you should also treat it with a sealant to protect it from the sun and rain.

Question 4: How can I make my garden furniture more comfortable?
Answer: There are a few things you can do to make your garden furniture more comfortable. You can add cushions and pillows to make it softer. You can also add a throw blanket to keep you warm on cooler evenings.

Question 5: How can I store my garden furniture?
Answer: If you do not have a shed or garage to store your garden furniture in, you can cover it with a tarp or plastic sheeting. You should also make sure to clean it before storing it to prevent mold and mildew from forming.

Question 6: How can I update my garden furniture?
Answer: There are a few things you can do to update your garden furniture. You can paint it a new color, add new cushions or pillows, or change the hardware. You can also add some new accessories, such as a throw blanket or a plant.
