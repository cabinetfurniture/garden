Repairs minor scratches

Black wax can also be used to repair minor scratches in your furniture. It is a simple and effective way to restore the beauty of your furniture without having to refinish it.

  • Clean the scratch

    Before you apply black wax to a scratch, it is important to clean the scratch with a damp cloth. This will remove any dirt or debris that could prevent the wax from adhering to the scratch.

  • Apply the black wax

    Once the scratch is clean, apply a small amount of black wax to the scratch using a soft cloth or brush. Rub the wax into the scratch in a circular motion.

  • Buff the wax

    Once you have applied the black wax to the scratch, buff it with a clean cloth. This will help to remove any excess wax and create a smooth, even finish.

  • Repeat as necessary

    If the scratch is deep, you may need to apply multiple coats of black wax. Allow each coat to dry completely before applying the next coat.
