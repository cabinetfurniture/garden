Question 1: What are the different types of garden furniture?
Answer: Garden furniture comes in a variety of styles, including traditional, modern, and contemporary. It can be made from a variety of materials, including wood, metal, plastic, and wicker.

Question 2: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer: The best material for garden furniture depends on your needs and preferences. Wood is a classic choice that is durable and stylish, but it requires regular maintenance. Metal is another durable option that is easy to clean, but it can be hot to the touch in the sun. Plastic is a lightweight and affordable option that is easy to care for, but it can be less durable than other materials.
