FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've got answers. Check out our frequently asked questions below:

Question 1: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer: The best material for garden furniture depends on your needs and preferences. Some popular materials include teak, acacia, and aluminum. Teak is a durable hardwood that is naturally resistant to rot and decay. Acacia is another durable hardwood that is also relatively affordable. Aluminum is a lightweight and rust-resistant metal that is easy to care for.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer: When choosing the right size garden furniture, it is important to consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain. You should also consider the size of your furniture in relation to the other elements in your outdoor space, such as your grill, fire pit, and plants.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer: The best way to care for your garden furniture is to clean it regularly with a mild soap and water solution. You should also protect your furniture from the elements by covering it when not in use. If your furniture is made of wood, you should also apply a sealant to protect it from the sun and rain.

Question 4: How do I store my garden furniture during the winter?
Answer: If you live in a climate with harsh winters, it is important to store your garden furniture indoors during the winter months. You can store your furniture in a garage, shed, or basement. Make sure to clean your furniture before storing it and cover it with a tarp or plastic wrap to protect it from dust and moisture.

Question 5: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Answer: Whether or not you can leave your garden furniture outside all year round depends on the climate you live in and the type of furniture you have. If you live in a mild climate, you may be able to leave your furniture outside all year round. However, if you live in a climate with harsh winters, it is best to store your furniture indoors during the winter months.

Question 6: How do I assemble my garden furniture?
Answer: Most garden furniture comes with detailed assembly instructions. Be sure to read the instructions carefully before beginning assembly. You may also need some basic tools, such as a screwdriver or wrench.
