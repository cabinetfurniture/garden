Outdoor Furniture Choosing the Right Material

Written by Christine Dec 23, 2023 Â· 12 min read

The right outdoor furniture can transform your backyard, patio, or deck into a comfortable and stylish outdoor oasis. But when it comes to choosing outdoor furniture, the material can make all the difference. With so many different options available, it can be hard to know where to start.

In this article, we'll break down the different types of outdoor furniture materials and their pros and cons to help you make the best decision for your needs and lifestyle.

Let's dive into the options!

Outdoor Furniture Material

Choosing the right material for your outdoor furniture is essential for ensuring its durability, comfort, and style. Here are 9 important points to consider when selecting a material:

  • Weather Resistance
  • Durability
  • Comfort
  • Maintenance
  • Style
  • Price
  • Sustainability
  • Weight
  • Availability

By considering these factors, you can choose the outdoor furniture material that best meets your needs and creates a comfortable and stylish outdoor space.

Weather Resistance

Weather resistance is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing outdoor furniture material. After all, your furniture will be exposed to the elements, so it needs to be able to withstand rain, sun, snow, and wind without deteriorating.

  • Durability: Weather-resistant materials are more durable and will last longer than materials that are not weather-resistant. This is important if you want your furniture to last for many years to come.
  • Colorfastness: Weather-resistant materials are also colorfast, meaning they will not fade or discolor when exposed to the sun. This is important if you want your furniture to retain its original appearance.
  • Resistance to moisture: Weather-resistant materials are resistant to moisture, meaning they will not absorb water and become damaged. This is important if you live in a humid climate or if you want to use your furniture near a pool or other body of water.
  • Resistance to pests: Some weather-resistant materials are also resistant to pests, such as insects and rodents. This is important if you want to avoid having to deal with pests damaging your furniture.

By choosing a weather-resistant material for your outdoor furniture, you can ensure that your furniture will last for many years to come and will continue to look its best.

Durability

Durability is another important factor to consider when choosing outdoor furniture material. After all, you want your furniture to last for many years to come, even if it is exposed to the elements and used frequently.

  • Resistance to wear and tear: Durable materials are resistant to wear and tear, meaning they will not easily scratch, dent, or tear. This is important if you have children or pets who will be using the furniture.
  • Strength: Durable materials are also strong, meaning they can withstand being used without breaking or collapsing. This is important if you plan on using your furniture for heavy-duty purposes, such as entertaining or hosting large gatherings.
  • Resistance to fading: Durable materials are also resistant to fading, meaning they will not lose their color over time when exposed to the sun. This is important if you want your furniture to retain its original appearance.
  • Easy to clean: Durable materials are also easy to clean, meaning you can keep them looking their best with minimal effort. This is important if you want to keep your furniture looking its best for many years to come.

By choosing a durable material for your outdoor furniture, you can ensure that your furniture will last for many years to come and will continue to look its best.

Comfort

Comfort is an important factor to consider when choosing outdoor furniture material, especially if you plan on spending a lot of time relaxing in your outdoor space. You want your furniture to be comfortable to sit or lie on, and you want it to provide good support for your body.

  • Cushioning: Cushioning can make a big difference in the comfort of your outdoor furniture. Look for furniture with cushions that are made from a durable, weather-resistant material and that provide good support for your back and neck.
  • Ergonomics: Ergonomic furniture is designed to support the natural curves of your body, which can help to reduce fatigue and discomfort. Look for furniture with ergonomic features, such as lumbar support and adjustable armrests.
  • Size: The size of your furniture is also important for comfort. Make sure to choose furniture that is the right size for your body and that provides enough space to relax and move around comfortably.
  • Material: The material of your furniture can also affect its comfort. For example, metal furniture can be uncomfortable to sit on in hot weather, while wicker furniture can be uncomfortable to sit on in cold weather.

By considering these factors, you can choose outdoor furniture that is comfortable and supportive, and that will allow you to relax and enjoy your outdoor space to the fullest.

Maintenance

Maintenance is an important factor to consider when choosing outdoor furniture material. After all, you want your furniture to last for many years to come, and you don't want to spend a lot of time and effort maintaining it.

  • Easy to clean: Choose furniture that is easy to clean. This will save you time and effort in the long run.
  • Low-maintenance: Choose furniture that is low-maintenance. This means that it will not require a lot of special care or treatment to keep it looking its best.
  • Resistant to stains and fading: Choose furniture that is resistant to stains and fading. This will help to keep your furniture looking its best for longer.
  • Durable: Choose furniture that is durable and will last for many years to come. This will save you money in the long run by not having to replace your furniture frequently.

By considering these factors, you can choose outdoor furniture that is easy to maintain and will last for many years to come.

Style

Style is an important factor to consider when choosing outdoor furniture material. After all, you want your furniture to match the overall style of your outdoor space and your personal taste.

There are many different styles of outdoor furniture material to choose from, so you can find something that fits your taste and needs. For example, if you want a classic look, you might choose wicker or wood furniture. If you want a more modern look, you might choose metal or plastic furniture.

In addition to the overall style of your furniture, you should also consider the specific details of the material. For example, if you choose wicker furniture, you should decide whether you want natural wicker or synthetic wicker. If you choose wood furniture, you should decide what type of wood you want and what finish you want.

By taking the time to consider your style and preferences, you can choose outdoor furniture material that you love and that will make your outdoor space look its best.

Price

Price is an important factor to consider when choosing outdoor furniture material. After all, you want to find furniture that fits your budget.

  • Material cost: The cost of the material is a major factor in the overall price of your outdoor furniture. Some materials, such as wood and metal, are more expensive than others, such as plastic and wicker.
  • Construction cost: The construction cost is another factor that can affect the price of your outdoor furniture. Furniture that is made with complex or intricate designs will be more expensive than furniture that is made with simple designs.
  • Brand name: The brand name can also affect the price of your outdoor furniture. Furniture from well-known brands will be more expensive than furniture from lesser-known brands.
  • Sales and discounts: Sales and discounts can help you save money on your outdoor furniture. Be sure to shop around and compare prices before you buy.

By considering these factors, you can find outdoor furniture that fits your budget and your needs.

Sustainability

Sustainability is an important factor to consider when choosing outdoor furniture material. After all, you want to choose furniture that is made from materials that are environmentally friendly and that will not harm the environment.

  • Recycled materials: Choose furniture that is made from recycled materials. This will help to reduce the amount of waste in landfills and conserve natural resources.
  • Sustainable materials: Choose furniture that is made from sustainable materials. Sustainable materials are those that are grown or harvested in a way that does not damage the environment.
  • FSC-certified wood: Choose furniture that is made from FSC-certified wood. FSC certification ensures that the wood has been harvested from sustainably managed forests.
  • Low-VOC materials: Choose furniture that is made from low-VOC materials. VOCs are volatile organic compounds that can contribute to air pollution and health problems.

By considering these factors, you can choose outdoor furniture that is sustainable and environmentally friendly.

Weight

Weight is an important factor to consider when choosing outdoor furniture material, especially if you plan on moving your furniture around frequently. You want to choose furniture that is light enough to move easily, but heavy enough to stay in place when you are using it.

The weight of your furniture will depend on the material it is made from. For example, metal furniture is typically heavier than plastic furniture. Wood furniture can be either heavy or light, depending on the type of wood used.

If you plan on moving your furniture around frequently, you should choose a material that is lightweight and easy to move. For example, plastic or aluminum furniture is a good choice for lightweight furniture that is easy to move.

If you want furniture that is more permanent and will not move around easily, you can choose a material that is heavier. For example, metal or wood furniture is a good choice for heavy furniture that will not move around easily.

Availability

Availability is an important factor to consider when choosing outdoor furniture material. You want to choose a material that is readily available and easy to find. This will make it easier for you to find replacement parts or additional pieces in the future.

Some materials are more widely available than others. For example, plastic and metal furniture are typically more widely available than wood furniture. This is because plastic and metal are more common materials that are used in a variety of applications.
