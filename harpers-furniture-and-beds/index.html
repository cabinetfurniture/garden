London-based heritage

Harpers Furniture and Beds is proudly rooted in the heart of London, a city renowned for its rich cultural heritage and exquisite craftsmanship. The company's story began in the early 1900s, when a young upholsterer named John Harper established a small workshop in the bustling East End.

Over the decades, Harpers gradually expanded its operations, becoming one of London's most respected furniture makers. Their reputation for exceptional craftsmanship and unwavering quality spread throughout the city and beyond.

Today, Harpers Furniture and Beds remains a London-based company, with its headquarters and flagship store located in the vibrant district of Clerkenwell. The company's deep connection to the city is evident in its designs, which often incorporate subtle nods to London's iconic architecture and cultural landmarks.

By choosing Harpers Furniture and Beds, you not only invest in beautiful and comfortable furnishings but also support a company that is an integral part of London's rich heritage.
