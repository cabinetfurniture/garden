FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've got you covered with our comprehensive FAQ section.

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer: The best materials for garden furniture are those that can withstand the elements, such as teak, acacia, and aluminum.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer: Measure the space where you plan to put your furniture and choose pieces that are appropriately sized. Consider the number of people you need to accommodate and the type of activities you'll be using the furniture for.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer: Regular cleaning and maintenance will help extend the life of your garden furniture. Clean it with a mild soap and water solution, and apply a protective sealant or oil as needed.

Question 4: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Answer: It depends on the material. Teak and acacia can withstand the elements, but other materials, such as aluminum and wicker, may need to be stored indoors during harsh weather.

Question 5: What are some tips for creating a stylish garden space?
Answer: Choose furniture that complements your home's exterior and the overall style of your garden. Add cushions and throws for comfort and color, and consider incorporating plants and other decorative elements.
