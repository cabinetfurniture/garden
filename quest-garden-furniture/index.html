What is the best way to clean my garden furniture?
The best way to clean your garden furniture is to use a mild soap and water solution. Rinse the furniture thoroughly with clean water and dry it completely with a soft cloth.

Can I leave my garden furniture outside in the rain?
We do not recommend leaving your garden furniture outside in the rain for extended periods of time. Rainwater can cause the furniture to rust or mildew. If you must leave your furniture outside in the rain, cover it with a tarp or store it in a covered area.

How often should I re-seal my garden furniture?
We recommend re-sealing your garden furniture every 6-12 months, or more often if it is exposed to harsh weather conditions. Re-sealing will help to protect the furniture from UV damage and water damage.

What is the best way to store my garden furniture during the off-season?
The best way to store your garden furniture during the off-season is to cover it with a tarp or store it in a covered area. If possible, elevate the furniture off the ground to prevent it from coming into contact with moisture.
