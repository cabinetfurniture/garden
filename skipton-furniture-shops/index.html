Here are some tips for spotting quality craftsmanship in furniture:

  • Look for solid wood construction. Solid wood is more durable than particle board or MDF, and it will last longer.
  • Check the joinery. The joints should be tight and secure, and they should be reinforced with glue and screws.
  • Examine the upholstery. The upholstery should be made from high-quality fabric, and it should be securely attached to the frame.
  • Consider the hardware. The hardware should be made from durable materials like brass or stainless steel, and it should be securely attached.
