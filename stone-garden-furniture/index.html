Durable

One of the best things about stone garden furniture is its durability. Stone is a very strong and hard-wearing material, so it can withstand the elements and heavy use without showing signs of wear and tear. This makes stone garden furniture a great choice for areas that get a lot of traffic, such as patios, decks, and poolsides.

Stone is also resistant to rot, decay, and insects, so it will not deteriorate over time like wood or metal furniture can. This means that stone garden furniture can last for many years with minimal maintenance.

In addition to its durability, stone is also very easy to clean. Most stains can be removed with just soap and water, and stone does not require any special treatment or sealant to protect it from the elements.

Overall, stone garden furniture is a great choice for anyone who is looking for durable, low-maintenance furniture that will last for many years.

Stone garden furniture is also very versatile. It can be used to create a variety of different looks, from traditional to modern. Stone furniture can also be used in a variety of different settings, from formal gardens to casual patios.

Heavy

One of the things to keep in mind about stone garden furniture is that it is very heavy. This can make it difficult to move around, especially if you need to rearrange your furniture or store it away for the winter.

If you are planning to purchase stone garden furniture, it is important to make sure that you have a plan for how you are going to move it. You may need to hire a professional mover or rent a dolly to help you transport the furniture.

Once you have your stone garden furniture in place, it is important to be careful not to move it too often. The weight of the furniture can put stress on the joints and connections, which can lead to damage.

If you do need to move your stone garden furniture, be sure to lift it carefully and evenly. Do not drag or slide the furniture, as this can damage the surface.

Despite its weight, stone garden furniture is still a great choice for many people. It is durable, weather-resistant, and easy to clean. With proper care and maintenance, stone garden furniture can last for many years.
