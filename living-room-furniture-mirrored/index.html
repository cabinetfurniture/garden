Easy to clean

Mirrored living room furniture is also easy to clean. All you need is a damp cloth and a mild cleaner. Simply wipe down the mirrored surfaces to remove dust and dirt.

  • Use a damp cloth: When cleaning mirrored furniture, it's important to use a damp cloth. A dry cloth can scratch the mirrored surfaces.
  • Use a mild cleaner: You can use a mild cleaner, such as glass cleaner or dish soap, to clean mirrored furniture. Avoid using harsh cleaners, as these can damage the mirrored surfaces.
  • Wipe down the mirrored surfaces: Simply wipe down the mirrored surfaces with the damp cloth and mild cleaner. Be sure to wipe in the direction of the grain to avoid scratching the mirrored surfaces.
  • Dry the mirrored surfaces: Once you have wiped down the mirrored surfaces, dry them with a clean cloth. This will help to prevent streaks and water spots.
