One of the unique characteristics of cream oak furniture is that it becomes lighter in color over time. This is because the tannins in the wood oxidize and break down, resulting in a lighter color. The color change is most noticeable in new cream oak furniture. As the furniture ages, the color will become more consistent throughout. The final color of the wood will depend on the amount of sunlight it is exposed to, as well as the age of the wood.

Tersedia dalam berbagai corak dan hasil akhir

Cream oak furniture is available in a variety of corak and hasil akhir, so you can find the perfect piece to match your style and décor. Corak refers to the natural grain pattern of the wood, while hasil akhir refers to the finish that is applied to the wood.

There are many different types of corak available in cream oak furniture. Some of the most popular corak include:

* **Straight grain:** This is the most common type of corak, and it features straight, parallel lines. * **Quarter sawn:** This type of corak is created by cutting the wood at a 90-degree angle to the grain, resulting in a more uniform and consistent look. * **Rift sawn:** This type of corak is created by cutting the wood at a 45-degree angle to the grain, resulting in a more pronounced and varied grain pattern.

There are also many different types of hasil akhir available for cream oak furniture. Some of the most popular hasil akhir include:

* **Clear finish:** This type of hasil akhir allows the natural color and grain of the wood to show through. * **Stained finish:** This type of hasil akhir adds a color to the wood, while still allowing the grain to show through. * **Painted finish:** This type of hasil akhir completely covers the wood with a paint color.

When choosing a corak and hasil akhir for your cream oak furniture, it is important to consider the style of your home and the other furniture in the room. You should also consider the amount of light in the room, as this will affect the way the wood looks.
