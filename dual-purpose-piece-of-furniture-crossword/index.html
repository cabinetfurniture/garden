When looking for furniture, you may come across terms like "dual-purpose" or "multi-purpose." These terms refer to furniture pieces that can be used for more than one purpose. For example, a dual-purpose piece of furniture could be a bed that also functions as a sofa. This type of furniture is often used in small spaces where there is not enough room for separate pieces of furniture for each purpose.

Dual-purpose furniture can be a great way to save space and money. It can also be a good way to add style to your home. If you are looking for a way to maximize the use of your space, consider investing in a dual-purpose piece of furniture.
