Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Caring for your garden furniture is easy. Regular cleaning with a mild soap solution will maintain its appearance. For wooden furniture, occasional oiling or sealing may be necessary.

Yes, our garden furniture is designed to withstand outdoor elements. However, we recommend storing cushions and fabrics indoors during inclement weather.
