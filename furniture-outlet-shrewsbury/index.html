FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've compiled a list of frequently asked questions and their answers to help you make informed decisions when choosing outdoor furniture for your garden.

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer 1: The best materials for garden furniture depend on your specific needs and preferences. Wood, metal, and plastic are popular choices. Wood offers a classic look and natural warmth, but requires regular maintenance. Metal is durable and weather-resistant, but can be prone to rust if not properly cared for. Plastic is lightweight, low-maintenance, and available in a variety of colors and designs.

Question 2: How do I choose comfortable garden furniture?
Answer 2: Comfort is key when it comes to garden furniture. Look for pieces with cushions or padded seats for added comfort. Consider the ergonomics of the chairs and tables to ensure they provide proper support and relaxation.

Question 3: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer 3: To protect your garden furniture from sun, rain, and other harsh weather conditions, consider using protective covers or storing the furniture indoors when not in use. Regular cleaning and maintenance can also help extend the lifespan of your garden furniture.

Question 4: What size garden furniture should I choose?
Answer 4: The size of your garden furniture should be proportionate to the size of your garden. Measure the available space before purchasing furniture to ensure that it fits comfortably without overcrowding the area.

Question 5: How can I create a cohesive look with my garden furniture?
Answer 5: To create a cohesive look with your garden furniture, choose pieces that complement each other in terms of style, color, and material. Consider adding outdoor rugs, cushions, and other accessories to tie the look together.

Question 6: Where can I find high-quality garden furniture at affordable prices?
Answer 6: You can find high-quality garden furniture at affordable prices at our furniture outlet in Shrewsbury. We offer a wide selection of garden furniture pieces made from durable materials and designed to withstand outdoor conditions, all at competitive prices.

Tips

Here are some practical tips to help you make the most of your garden furniture and create a beautiful and inviting outdoor space:

Tip 1: Choose the Right Location:

When placing your garden furniture, consider factors such as sunlight, shade, and traffic flow. Position your furniture in areas that receive a balance of sun and shade throughout the day. Make sure there is enough space for people to move around comfortably and access the furniture easily.

Tip 2: Create a Focal Point:

Create a focal point in your garden to draw the eye and add interest. This could be a stunning piece of garden furniture, such as a statement chair or a unique table, or a natural element like a water feature or a blooming flower bed.

Tip 3: Add Comfort and Style:

Enhance the comfort and style of your garden furniture with cushions, throws, and outdoor rugs. Choose weather-resistant fabrics and materials that can withstand the elements. Add decorative pillows and throws to create a cozy and inviting atmosphere.

Tip 4: Maintain Your Garden Furniture:

Regular maintenance is essential to extend the lifespan of your garden furniture and keep it looking its best. Clean your furniture regularly with mild soap and water, and apply protective treatments or sealants as recommended by the manufacturer. Store cushions and other accessories indoors during inclement weather to prevent damage.
