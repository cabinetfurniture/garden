Choose the right material. Garden furniture is made from a variety of materials, including wood, metal, plastic, and wicker. Each material has its own advantages and disadvantages. Wood is durable and stylish, but it requires regular maintenance. Metal is durable and weather-resistant, but it can be heavy and hot to the touch. Plastic is lightweight and weather-resistant, but it can be flimsy and fade over time. Wicker is lightweight and stylish, but it is not as durable as other materials. Consider your climate. If you live in a climate with extreme weather conditions, you will need to choose furniture that is durable enough to withstand the elements. For example, if you live in a rainy climate, you will need to choose furniture that is water-resistant. If you live in a sunny climate, you will need to choose furniture that is UV-resistant.
