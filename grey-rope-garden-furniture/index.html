All-weather resistant

Grey rope garden furniture is designed to withstand the elements, making it an ideal choice for outdoor spaces in all climates.

  • UV resistant:

    Grey rope garden furniture is treated with UV inhibitors to protect it from the sun's harmful rays. This prevents the color from fading and the material from becoming brittle over time.

  • Water-resistant:

    The tight weave of the rope prevents water from seeping through, making it quick-drying and resistant to rain and moisture. This also makes it easy to clean, as you can simply hose it down or wipe it with a damp cloth.

  • Wind-resistant:

Thanks to its all-weather resistant properties, grey rope garden furniture can be enjoyed year-round, regardless of the weather conditions.
