Choosing the Right Rectangular Garden Table Cover

Written by Aprilia Apr 30, 2025 Â· 12 min read

Protecting your garden furniture from the elements is essential for extending its lifespan and maintaining its appearance. Rectangular garden tables, in particular, require a cover that fits snugly to prevent wind damage and water ingress. This article will guide you through the key considerations when choosing a rectangular garden table cover, ensuring your table stays protected and looking its best.

The first step is to determine the exact dimensions of your rectangular garden table. Measure the length, width, and height, including any overhang or extensions. These measurements will help you select a cover that provides a precise fit, preventing it from flapping in the wind or leaving exposed areas.

With the dimensions in hand, you can now explore the different types of rectangular garden table covers available. Several materials offer varying levels of durability, breathability, and UV protection, so it's important to consider your specific needs.

Choosing the right rectangular garden table cover is essential to protect your furniture from the elements and extend its lifespan. Here are six important points to consider when making your decision:

  • Material: Choose a durable and weather-resistant material such as polyester or vinyl.
  • Dimensions: Measure your table carefully to ensure a snug fit.
  • Shape: Select a cover specifically designed for rectangular tables.
  • Breathability: Opt for a breathable material to prevent moisture buildup.
  • UV protection: Choose a cover with UV protection to prevent fading.
  • Features: Consider additional features such as drawstrings, zippers, or vents.

By following these tips, you can choose the right rectangular garden table cover to keep your furniture protected and looking its best.

Material: Choose a durable and weather-resistant material such as polyester or vinyl.

The material of your rectangular garden table cover plays a crucial role in its durability and weather resistance. Here are four key points to consider when choosing a material:

  • Polyester: Polyester is a synthetic material known for its strength, durability, and resistance to fading. It is also water-repellent and easy to clean.

Details: Polyester covers are a popular choice for rectangular garden table covers due to their combination of durability, weather resistance, and affordability.

Vinyl: Vinyl is another durable and weather-resistant material. It is waterproof, UV-resistant, and resistant to tearing and punctures.

Details: Vinyl covers are a good choice for areas with harsh weather conditions or for tables that are exposed to heavy use.

Oxford fabric: Oxford fabric is a durable and water-resistant fabric that is often used for outdoor furniture covers. It is made from polyester or nylon and has a special coating that makes it resistant to fading and mildew.

Details: Oxford fabric covers are a good choice for rectangular garden table covers that are exposed to moderate weather conditions.

Canvas: Canvas is a heavy-duty fabric that is made from cotton or linen. It is water-resistant, UV-resistant, and resistant to tearing and punctures.

Details: Canvas covers are a good choice for rectangular garden table covers that are exposed to heavy use or harsh weather conditions.

By choosing a durable and weather-resistant material, you can ensure that your rectangular garden table cover will protect your furniture from the elements and extend its lifespan.

Details: The width of your cover should be at least as wide as your table, but again, it is better to get a cover that is slightly wider to ensure a good overlap.

Breathability: Opt for a breathable material to prevent moisture buildup.

Choosing a breathable material for your rectangular garden table cover is important to prevent moisture buildup, which can lead to mold and mildew growth. Here are four key points to consider when selecting a breathable material:

  • Breathable materials allow air to circulate, which helps to prevent moisture from accumulating under the cover. This is especially important in humid climates or during periods of heavy rain.

UV protection: Choose a cover with UV protection to prevent fading.

UV protection is an important feature to consider when choosing a rectangular garden table cover. UV rays from the sun can cause the color of your table to fade over time, making it look old and worn. A cover with UV protection will help to prevent this fading and keep your table looking its best for longer.

There are two main types of UV protection: UV absorbers and UV blockers. UV absorbers absorb UV rays and convert them into heat, while UV blockers reflect UV rays away from the surface of the cover. Both types of UV protection can be effective in preventing fading.

When choosing a cover with UV protection, it is important to consider the level of UV protection that you need. If you live in an area with high levels of UV radiation, you will need a cover with a high level of UV protection. If you live in an area with lower levels of UV radiation, you may be able to get away with a cover with a lower level of UV protection.

You can also find covers with additional features, such as water resistance and breathability. These features can help to protect your table from the elements and keep it dry and protected.

By choosing a cover with UV protection, you can help to prevent fading and keep your rectangular garden table looking its best for longer.

Features: Consider additional features such as drawstrings, zippers, or vents.

Drawstrings are a common feature on rectangular garden table covers. Drawstrings allow you to cinch the cover tightly around the table, which helps to prevent the cover from blowing off in the wind. Drawstrings also make it easy to remove the cover when you need to access your table.

Zippers are another popular feature on rectangular garden table covers. Zippers allow you to easily remove and replace the cover, which can be helpful for cleaning or maintenance. Zippers also make it easy to access your table without having to remove the entire cover.
