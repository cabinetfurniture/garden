Easy maintenance

Royalcraft's garden furniture is designed to be easy to maintain, allowing you to spend more time enjoying your outdoor space and less time on upkeep. Our furniture pieces are crafted from materials that require minimal care and can be easily cleaned to keep them looking their best.

Teak wood, a popular choice for outdoor furniture, is naturally resistant to rot and decay, making it low-maintenance. To maintain its beautiful appearance, teak furniture can be occasionally cleaned with a mild soap solution and allowed to dry naturally.

Stainless steel is another low-maintenance material that is resistant to rust and corrosion. To clean stainless steel furniture, simply wipe it down with a damp cloth and mild soap, and dry it thoroughly.

Powder-coated aluminum is also easy to maintain. The powder coating provides a durable finish that is resistant to scratches and fading. To clean powder-coated aluminum furniture, simply wipe it down with a damp cloth and mild soap, and dry it thoroughly.
