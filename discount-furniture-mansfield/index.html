  • Cedar: Cedar is another type of wood that is naturally resistant to rot and decay. It is also a good choice for garden furniture that will be exposed to the weather.
  • Acacia: Acacia is a type of wood that is durable and easy to care for. It is a good choice for garden furniture that will be used frequently.
  • Plastic: Polyethylene furniture is a low-maintenance option for garden furniture. It is durable, weather-proof, and easy to keep clean.
  • Wicker: Wicker furniture is made from natural materials, such as rattan or wicker. It is lightweight and durable, and it adds a touch of style to any garden.
