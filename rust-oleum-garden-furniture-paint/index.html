Tips for Using Rust-Matic Garden Paint

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best results when using Rust-Matic Garden Paint:

1. Prepare the surface properly
Before applying Rust-Matic Garden Paint, make sure the surface is clean, dry, and free of any loose or peeling paint. If necessary, lightly sand the surface to create a smooth and even surface for the paint to adhere to.

2. Use a primer
Applying a primer before painting can help to improve the adhesion of the paint and extend its durability. Rust-Matic offers a dedicated primer that is specially formulated to be used with Rust-Matic Garden Paint.

3. Apply thin coats
Rather than applying one thick coat of paint, it's better to apply two or three thin coats. This will help to prevent runs and drips, and it will also allow the paint to dry more evenly.

4. Allow ample drying time
Be sure to allow each coat of paint to dry completely before applying the next coat. This will help to ensure that the paint cures properly and forms a durable finish.

By following these tips, you can achieve a professional-looking finish on your garden furniture that will last for years to come.
