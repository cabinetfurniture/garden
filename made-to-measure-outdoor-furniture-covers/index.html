Custom fit

A custom fit is paramount for furniture covers, ensuring they stay securely in place, protecting your furniture from the elements and other hazards. Made-to-measure covers are tailored to the exact dimensions of your furniture, providing a snug and secure fit that prevents wind, rain, and dirt from seeping through.

  • Precise measurements: Made-to-measure covers are designed to fit your furniture like a glove, eliminating excess fabric that can flap in the wind or pool water. This precision ensures maximum protection and a tailored look.
  • No more shifting: With a custom fit, the cover will not shift or slide off, even in strong winds or heavy rain. This prevents damage to your furniture and ensures it stays protected at all times.
  • Protection from all angles: A custom fit ensures that all sides of your furniture are fully covered, leaving no gaps for water, dirt, or debris to enter. This comprehensive protection extends the life of your furniture and keeps it looking its best.
  • Aesthetic appeal: A well-fitted cover complements the design of your furniture, enhancing the overall aesthetic of your outdoor space. Custom-made covers can be tailored to match the style and color of your furniture, creating a cohesive and visually appealing look.

Investing in a custom-fit made-to-measure outdoor furniture cover not only protects your furniture but also adds a touch of style to your outdoor living area. Enjoy peace of mind knowing that your furniture is shielded from the elements and looking its best, season after season.

Weatherproof

Made-to-measure outdoor furniture covers are designed to withstand the elements, providing year-round protection for your furniture. These covers are typically crafted from durable, waterproof materials that can endure extreme weather conditions.

Here's how made-to-measure covers keep your furniture safe from the elements:

  • Rain and snow: Waterproof covers prevent rain and snow from seeping into your furniture, protecting it from moisture damage, mold, and mildew. They also keep your furniture dry and comfortable, ensuring you can enjoy it even after a downpour.
  • Wind: Made-to-measure covers are designed to stay securely in place, even in strong winds. The custom fit and durable materials prevent the cover from flapping or tearing, ensuring your furniture is shielded from wind damage.
  • Sun: Prolonged exposure to sunlight can damage and fade outdoor furniture. Made-to-measure covers with UV protection block harmful rays, preventing discoloration and premature aging of your furniture.
  • Extreme temperatures: Outdoor furniture covers are made from materials that can withstand extreme temperatures, both hot and cold. This protects your furniture from cracking, warping, or fading due to thermal fluctuations.

By investing in a weatherproof made-to-measure outdoor furniture cover, you can rest assured that your furniture is protected from the elements, extending its lifespan and maintaining its beauty for seasons to come.

Durable materials

Made-to-measure outdoor furniture covers are crafted from durable materials designed to withstand the rigors of outdoor use. These materials are chosen for their strength, resistance to wear and tear, and ability to protect your furniture from the elements.

  • Ripstop fabrics: Ripstop fabrics are woven with a special reinforced grid pattern that prevents tears from spreading. This makes them ideal for outdoor furniture covers, as they can withstand accidental snags or tears without compromising the integrity of the cover.
  • UV-resistant materials: Prolonged exposure to sunlight can damage and fade outdoor furniture. Made-to-measure covers are often made from UV-resistant materials that block harmful rays, preventing discoloration and premature aging of your furniture.
  • Waterproof coatings: Waterproof coatings are applied to the outer layer of the cover, creating a barrier that prevents rain and snow from seeping through. These coatings also protect your furniture from moisture damage, mold, and mildew.
  • Breathable fabrics: While it's important for outdoor furniture covers to be waterproof, they should also allow for breathability. Breathable fabrics prevent condensation from building up inside the cover, which can lead to mold and mildew growth.

By choosing a made-to-measure outdoor furniture cover made from durable materials, you can ensure that your furniture is protected from the elements and will last for many years to come.

  • Complements your furniture: A well-fitted made-to-measure cover will complement the design of your furniture, enhancing its overall appearance. By choosing a cover that matches the style and color of your furniture, you can create a sophisticated and inviting outdoor space.
  • Protects and beautifies: Made-to-measure covers not only protect your furniture from the elements, but they also add a touch of beauty to your outdoor space. Easy to clean

Made-to-measure outdoor furniture covers are designed to be easy to clean and maintain. Here are some of the features that make them a breeze to keep clean:

  • Machine washable: Many made-to-measure outdoor furniture covers are machine washable, making it easy to remove dirt, dust, and debris. Simply toss the cover in the washing machine on a gentle cycle and air dry it to refresh and clean it.
  • Wipe-clean surfaces: Some made-to-measure covers are made from materials that can be easily wiped clean with a damp cloth. This is a convenient option for quick cleanups or removing light dirt and spills.
  • Fade-resistant materials: Made-to-measure covers are often made from fade-resistant materials that prevent discoloration from sunlight and UV rays. This means that your cover will stay looking fresh and clean for longer, even after repeated washings or exposure to the elements.
  • Mold and mildew resistant: Made-to-measure covers are often treated with mold and mildew-resistant coatings to prevent the growth of these unsightly and potentially harmful microorganisms. This helps to keep your covers clean and fresh, even in humid or damp environments.

By choosing a made-to-measure outdoor furniture cover that is easy to clean, you can keep your furniture looking its best and extend its lifespan. Regular cleaning will remove dirt, debris, and other contaminants that can damage your furniture or cause fading and discoloration.

  • Protection from dirt and dust: Dirt and dust can accumulate on outdoor furniture, making it look dull and worn. Made-to-measure covers keep your furniture clean and protected from dirt, dust, and other environmental pollutants.
  • Protection from pests and animals: Pests and animals can damage outdoor furniture by chewing on it, nesting in it, or leaving droppings on it. Made-to-measure covers create a barrier between your furniture and these unwanted visitors, deterring them from causing damage.

Tips

Here are some practical tips to help you extend the life of your garden furniture:

  1. Clean your furniture regularly: Dirt and grime can damage your furniture over time, so it's important to clean it regularly. Use a mild soap solution and a soft cloth to wipe down your furniture, and be sure to dry it thoroughly afterwards.
  2. Protect your furniture from the elements: Sun, rain, and wind can take a toll on your garden furniture, so it's important to protect it from the elements when not in use. Use a made-to-measure cover or store your furniture in a shed or garage.
  3. Don't overload your furniture: Overloading your furniture can put stress on the frame and joints, leading to damage. Be sure to distribute weight均匀ly and avoid placing heavy objects on your furniture.
  4. Inspect your furniture regularly: Regularly inspect your garden furniture for any signs of damage, such as loose screws, cracks, or tears. By catching problems early, you can often prevent them from becoming more serious.
