Aluminum is a lightweight and rust-resistant metal that is easy to care for. Plastic is a budget-friendly option that is available in a variety of colors and styles.

Answer: When choosing the right size garden furniture, it is important to consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain.
