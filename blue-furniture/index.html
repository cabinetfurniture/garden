Tips

Once you've chosen the perfect garden furniture for your outdoor space, you'll want to make sure you take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

Tip 1: Clean your garden furniture regularly.

This will help to remove dirt and debris, and it will also help to prevent the furniture from fading or rusting. Depending on the material of your furniture, you can clean it with a mild detergent and water, or you can use a commercial cleaner specifically designed for garden furniture.

Tip 2: Store your garden furniture properly during the winter months.

If you live in an area with harsh winters, it is important to store your garden furniture properly to prevent it from being damaged by the cold weather. You can either store your furniture in a garage or shed, or you can cover it with a tarp.
