Easy to install

Furniture raising blocks are incredibly easy to install. They typically come with self-adhesive pads that can be attached to the bottom of the furniture legs. Once the pads are in place, simply place the furniture on top of the blocks and adjust the height as needed.

Here are the step-by-step instructions on how to install furniture raising blocks:

  1. Clean the bottom of the furniture legs to remove any dirt or debris.
  2. Peel off the backing of the self-adhesive pads and attach them to the bottom of the furniture legs.
  3. Place the furniture on top of the blocks and adjust the height as needed.
  4. Once the furniture is level, press down on each block to secure it in place.

That's it! Installing furniture raising blocks is a quick and easy process that can be completed in just a few minutes.
