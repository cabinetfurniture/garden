There are a few things to consider when choosing a furniture mover with wheels. First, consider the weight and size of the furniture you'll be moving. You'll want to choose a mover that is rated for the weight of your furniture, and that has a large enough platform to accommodate the size of your items.

Once you've chosen a furniture mover with wheels, it's time to get started. First, remove any loose items from the furniture, such as drawers or shelves. Then, position the mover under the furniture and lift it up slightly. Finally, tilt the furniture back and roll it away on the wheels.
