    Question 1: What is the best material for garden furniture?
    Answer: The best material for garden furniture is teak. Teak is a durable hardwood that is naturally resistant to rot and decay. It is also a beautiful wood with a rich grain. Other good materials for garden furniture include acacia, eucalyptus, and cedar.

    Answer: To care for your garden furniture, you should clean it regularly with a mild soap and water solution. You should also apply a sealant to the furniture every year to protect it from the elements.
