Written by Eveline Nov 14, 2024 · 16 min read

When choosing garden furniture, one of the most important decisions you'll make is the colour. The colour of your furniture can set the tone for your entire outdoor space, so it's important to choose carefully.

There are a few things to keep in mind when choosing a colour for your garden furniture. First, consider the style of your home and garden. If you have a traditional home, you may want to choose furniture in a classic colour like black or white. If you have a more modern home, you may want to choose furniture in a more vibrant colour like blue or green.

Next, think about the purpose of your garden furniture. If you're going to be using it for relaxing, you may want to choose a colour that is calming and inviting. If you're going to be using it for entertaining, you may want to choose a colour that is more lively and energetic.

Finally, consider the amount of sunlight your garden gets. If you have a sunny garden, you can choose furniture in a darker colour. If you have a shady garden, you may want to choose furniture in a lighter colour.

Consider the durability of the colour

When choosing a colour for your garden furniture, it's important to consider the durability of the colour. Some colours are more likely to fade or chip over time, especially if they are exposed to the elements.

  • Solid colours. Solid colours are generally more durable than patterns or prints. This is because there are no seams or lines where the colour can fade or chip.
  • Dark colours. Dark colours are also more durable than light colours. This is because they absorb more light and heat, which helps to prevent the colour from fading.
  • Matte finishes. Matte finishes are more durable than glossy finishes. This is because they are less likely to show scratches or wear.
  • Powder-coated finishes. Powder-coated finishes are very durable and resistant to fading and chipping. This makes them a good choice for garden furniture that will be exposed to the elements.

By following these tips, you can choose a colour for your garden furniture that is both durable and stylish.
