Variety of Finishes: Matte, Gloss, Satin.

Rust-Oleum furniture paint is available in a variety of finishes, including matte, gloss, and satin, so you can choose the perfect look for your project.

  • Matte:

    Matte finishes have a flat, non-reflective appearance. They are a good choice for furniture that you want to blend in with the surroundings or for a more rustic or industrial look.

  • Gloss:

    Gloss finishes have a high sheen and reflect light, making them a good choice for furniture that you want to make a statement with. Gloss finishes are also easier to clean than other finishes.

  • Satin:

    Satin finishes have a slight sheen, but they are not as reflective as gloss finishes. They are a good choice for furniture that you want to look elegant and sophisticated.

  • Other Finishes:

    In addition to matte, gloss, and satin, Rust-Oleum also offers a variety of specialty finishes, such as metallic, hammered, and crackle. These finishes can add a unique and stylish touch to your furniture.

The type of finish you choose will depend on your personal preference and the style of your furniture. If you're not sure which finish to choose, you can always test out a few different types on a small area of your furniture before you commit to painting the entire piece.

Tips
